What to expect

Planning your journey with New Era Tours is designed to be collaborative and stress-free. Here's what you need to know about how we work together to create your perfect Israeli experience.

How does the planning process work?

We start with a conversation about your interests, travel style, and must-see destinations. From there, we craft a detailed itinerary that balances iconic sites with hidden gems, building in flexibility for spontaneous discoveries along the way.

What are typical timelines?

For custom tours, we recommend reaching out 4-6 weeks before your travel dates to allow time for thoughtful planning and securing the best guides. Last-minute requests are welcome when availability permits, and we'll do our best to accommodate your schedule.

Do you accommodate accessibility needs?

Absolutely. Israel has made significant strides in accessibility, and we work closely with you to ensure sites, transportation, and accommodations meet your specific requirements. Let us know your needs upfront so we can plan accordingly.

What pace should I expect?

We design tours that balance immersive experiences with downtime. Whether you prefer a leisurely exploration or a packed itinerary, we'll match the rhythm to your energy level and interests, always leaving room to savor the moments that matter most.

Do you work with groups or just individuals?

We welcome both! From solo travelers and couples to family reunions and corporate groups, we tailor experiences for any size. Group dynamics are factored into the itinerary to ensure everyone has a meaningful and cohesive journey.

What information helps you plan best?

The more we know, the better we can customize. Share your travel dates, interests (history, food, nature, spirituality), mobility considerations, dietary needs, and any specific sites or experiences you're dreaming of. Even small details help us craft something truly special.
